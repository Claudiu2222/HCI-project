1. Abstraction

GUD Boy is a project that aims to help people care for their dogs. The project will provide users with a platform where they can keep track of their dog's health, schedule vet appointments, and receive reminders for vaccinations. The platform will also provide users with information about dog breeds, training tips, and other useful dog care information. Also, there is a possibility to get in touch with a certified trainer for a personalized training plan.

2. Interaction desing patterns for Mobile platforms.

2.1 Navigation menu with quick access items

This pattern presents a main menu that contains buttons or texts for different sections of the application. Users can quickly access the sections they want without having to navigate through a series of screens or additional menus.

Features of navigation menu with quick access items:

Button and text for the application sections: here we have several elements where the user can access and navigate among the features of the application

Clear and concise view: menu items are present in a clear and concise manner so that users they can quickly identify the section they want to access.

Logical order: Menu items are placed in a logical order so users can easily find the section they need.

Visual feedback: We provide visual feedback when the user selects a menu option, an animation for what the selection indicates.

Direct navigation: Ensures that users can access items directly from the main menu without having to pass through other navigation levels. This design pattern provides users with a simple and effective way to to access items from the app's main menu, allowing them to manage and view their schedule without additional effort.

2.2 Dog health section

The dog health section is designed to help users keep track of their dog's health and schedule vet appointments. Users can add their dog's information, such as age, weight, and breed, and receive reminders for vaccinations and check-ups. The section also provides users with information about common health issues in dogs and how to prevent them. The dog health section is designed to be easy to use and informative, with clear instructions and visuals to help users understand the information.

Patterns used:

Card layout pattern

This pattern is suitable for displaying dog grooming lessons as cards, each card containing information and details about a specific lesson. Users can easily navigate through the cards and quickly access the information they want.

Features of card layout pattern:

Structured view: Each card presents a specific dog care lesson or concept, structured in a clear and concise format.

Quick access to information: Users can quickly access information of interest without having to navigate through multiple levels of menus or screens.

Intuitive Navigation: Cards are arranged in a logical order and can be scrolled or swiped in an intuitive way, allowing users to find the desired lessons easily.

Timeline pattern

This pattern is suitable for displaying the dog's medical history, showing events and doctor visits in chronological order. Users can quickly view their dog's health progress and history.

Features of Timeline Pattern:

Chronological Presentation: Events and doctor visits are displayed in chronological order, giving users a clear view of the dog's medical history.

Details Contents: Each event in the history can be expanded to show additional details such as diagnosis, treatment and other observations.

Interactivity: Users can interact with events in history to add or edit information, or to filter events based on specific criteria. These design patterns provide an efficient and intuitive way to organize and present your dog's health and care information in the app your. Their use can improve the user experience and efficiency in managing their dog's health.

2.3 Calendar view pattern

This pattern presents data in the form of a traditional calendar, with the days of the week arranged in rows and columns. Users can navigate between different months and years to view and manage their events. Users can set certain tasks that they have to do for their dogs.

Features of Calendar View Pattern: Monthly or Weekly View: Users can select between monthly or weekly view depending on their preferences or planning needs.

Display events: Events are displayed on the calendar on the corresponding days, either by color or icon-uir, to indicate the type of task to be performed observed or scheduled time.

Interactivity: Users can interact with events to edit, delete or add new events directly from the calendar. Easy Navigation: There are intuitive navigation options, such as arrows to move between months or buttons to quickly return to the current date.

Compact and expanded view mode: Users can select between compact or expanded view modes, depending on the amount of information they want to see on the screen. This design pattern provides a familiar and intuitive way for users to manage their daily tasks and schedule, allowing them to see and manage their activities in a structured and organized form.

2.4 Lessons

The lessons section is designed to help users learn more about their dogs and how to care for them. The lessons are divided into different categories, such as health, training, and nutrition. Users can browse through the lessons and read more about each topic. The lessons are designed to be interactive and engaging, with images and videos to help users understand the information better.

Patterns used:

Card pattern

The card pattern is used to display the lessons in an organized and visually appealing way. Each lesson is displayed as a card, with an image, title, and short description. Users can click on the card to read more about the lesson.

Action button

The action button is used to allow users to take action on the lesson, such as reading more about it or sharing it with others. The action button is placed at the bottom of the card, making it easy for users to find and interact with. Also, on the lesson view, you can schedule the lesson.

2.5 Community

The community section is designed to allow users to connect with other dog owners and trainers and share their experiences, stories, training tips.

Patterns Used:

Card pattern

Each community post is presented on a card that includes visual content, a headline, a summary, and interactive buttons such as 'Like', and 'Comment'.

Action buttons

These buttons facilitate community interactions directly from the post cards, enabling users to share posts, participate in discussions, and connect with other members.

Voice command

Community members can use voice commands to quickly search for topics, navigate through posts.

Date and time

All posts, comments, and messages display the date and time of posting or sending, ensuring users are aware of the timing of interactions.

Author and avatar

Add Emoji/Reaction

Users can quickly respond to posts and messages with emojis, facilitating easier and faster interactions.

Upload Media

Users can upload photos and videos directly in their posts or messages.

Rating

Users can rate trainers based on their interactions or advice

Create groups

Trainers and users can create special interest groups

2.6 Virtual meetings

The virtual meetings feature facilitates real-time interactions between community members through voice and video communication. This section is essential for conducting remote training sessions, holding Q&A with experts, and fostering real-time dialogues among users.

Patterns Used:

Voice pattern

Auditory feedback confirms actions within the meetings, such as connection status and microphone toggling.

Menus

Menus provide easy access to meeting controls, including participant settings, screen sharing options, and camera adjustments

Buttons, images, labels

These elements are used to facilitate interactions within the meeting, such as muting/unmuting, sharing screens, and adjusting camera settings.

Sender/Receiver message

This pattern is used to display messages sent and received during the meeting, allowing users to follow the conversation and participate in real-time discussions.

3. Interaction desing patterns for AR platforms.

3.1 AR Overlay pattern

This pattern allows for the overlay of information, images, or virtual elements onto the real world seen through AR devices, such as augmented reality glasses.

Features of the AR Overlay Pattern:

Real-time Information Overlay: While the user views the real world through AR glasses, information or recommendations can be overlaid onto the user's field of view to provide guidance and instructions in real-time. Interactivity with

Virtual Objects: Users can interact with objects or virtual dogs present in the AR world, using gestures or voice commands to interact with them. Virtual Pet Simulation Pattern This pattern enables users to interact with a virtual dog in a simulated environment, experiencing different situations and scenarios to understand and improve the reactions and behavior of their real dogs.

Features of the Virtual Pet Simulation Pattern:

Simulation of Real Situations: Users can place a headset on their real dog's head and expose it to different stimuli or virtual situations to observe and understand its reactions.

Real-time Feedback: Users receive real-time feedback about the behavior and reactions of their real dogs in relation to the simulated virtual situations, allowing them to understand and improve their interaction with their dogs. Virtual Training Pattern This pattern allows users to conduct virtual training sessions with virtual dogs, providing instructions and guidance in real-time to improve the training and behavior skills of their real dogs.

Features of the Virtual Training Pattern:

Virtual Instructions and Exercises: Users can access different training exercises and instructions presented in the form of virtual activities with virtual dogs.

Real-time Feedback and Corrections: Users receive real-time feedback and corrections on how they train their dogs, allowing them to improve their technique and achieve better results. By implementing these design patterns in the Augmented Reality section of your application, you will provide users with an engaging and interactive experience, allowing them to experiment with and improve their interactions with their dogs in a safe and controlled virtual environment.
