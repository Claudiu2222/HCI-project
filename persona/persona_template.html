Name: Razvan Paul Olariu

Background
  • Date of Birth: 21/05/1989
  • Gender: Male
  • Location: Vaslui, Romania
  • Work place: Grapefruit, digital strategist
  • School: UAIC, Geografie
  • Technology Level:High
Main Points
  • Razvan is scared by the difficulties he encountered in training Django, in particular by the situations in which classical methods did not resonate for his dog
  • As a digital strategist, Razvan's job requires him to be tech-savvy and efficient in managing his time. He seeks tools and solutions that seamlessly integrate into his busy lifestyle and provide practical assistance.
  • In his professional life Razvan works with people and he likes it, he will also enjoys interacting with other dog owners and values the opportunity to learn from their experience
  • Razvan wants to be a confident dog owner, who can offer happiness and a good education to his friend
Goals
  • Razvan's primary goal is to ensure Django's happiness and well-being, which includes providing proper education and training for his dog.
  • Being a strong and confident dog owner
  • Better time management, saving time in training the dog
Frustrations and Pain Points
  • A main problem could be related to Razvan's patience in this education process
  • A main problem could be related to the explanations given by the coach
  • A problem could be related to the UI of how the application interface is built and how easy or hard it seems
  • Problems with the product: slowness, hard to use, no feedback provided
Detailed Description

Meet Razvan, he is a digital strategist passionate about nature, music, marketing and data analytics, but also our primary persona. From the beginning of his journey to adulthood, he adopted a playful and lively dog, Django.

In his busy life Razvan juggles between his job, social commitments and most importantly ensuring Django's happiness. As soon as he decided to adopt a dog, this came with an extra responsibility, but the most important aspect being its education.

Razvan also took care of Django's training, documenting himself and watching videos on the Internet. However, he encountered difficulties in finding the right approach that would resonate with Django in different situations.

With the appearance of the GUD BOI application, Razvan sees an opportunity to fulfill the ultimate goal of the dog education. He sees this application as a tool that not only provides guidance and feedback, but also one that fits the lifestyle.

The lessons and videos on the platform will help Razvan to understand certain things about Django, moreover, interacting with other users through the application excites him, being able to learn from the experiences of other dog owners. One thing that Razvan sees as innovative is the part of AR integrated in the application, in which he will be able to interact in 2 ways. In the first part he will be made to act in different simulated situations with a virtual dog, from here he will learn how to manage these situations in real life together with Django. As for the second part, he will receive certain recommendations regarding the actions he takes on the dog in real time and not simulated.

Razvan's goals with the GUD BOI app are varied. On a practical level, they hope to improve Django's behavior and address specific behavioral issues such as leash pulling and jumping. Personally, he wants to feel confident and strong as a dog owner, knowing that he is providing the best possible care for Django. From a business perspective, Razvan hopes the app will help her save time and effort in training Django, allowing her to focus on other aspects of her life and work.

Scenarios

A first scenario of use can be that of documenting and reading articles predisposed to the application. In the first phase, Razvan will have to learn a little about dog psychology and some basic elements in training.

A second scenario will be related to training a virtual puppy through AR, Razvan will need a headset through which he will do different tutorials in order to be prepared to put into practice what he learns when he faces situations similar

A third scenario will be related to real-time recommendations that Razvan will actually receive based on the actions he takes on Django.

Name: Carlos Mendez

Background
  • Date of Birth: 19/05/1985
  • Gender: Male
  • Location: Los Angeles, California, USA
  • Work place: Mendez Canine Academy, Trainer
  • Technology Level: Intermediate
Main Points
  • Widely recognized for his approach to dog training, focusing on the relationship between owner and dog;
  • He dislikes forceful training methods which can scare the dog for life;
  • Carlos does both one-on-one sessions and large seminars;
  • Uses social media and digital platforms to extend his influence in dog training;
  • Carlos wants to help dog owners establish good relationships with their dogs;
Goals
  • Practical Goals: Find a dog training app which will give him the tools to help out dog owners
  • Personal Goals: To be seen not just as a trainer but as an educator with his training methods
  • Business Goals: Expand his reach by working with an app which shares his vision
Frustrations and Pain Points
  • He finds that many apps have a clunky UI and poor user experience, which will make users stop using the app long-term.
  • Some apps spread misinformation about dog training
  • Carlos had difficulties finding an app which give insightful feedback to the users.
Detailed Description

Carlos Mendez is a dog trainer from Los Angeles, California who revolutionized the dog training with his empathic approach and his knowledge. Through his work, Carlos aims to help dog owners establish a strong bond with their pets, based on trust and understanding. His training methods are based on positive reinforcement and building a strong relationship between the owner and the dog.

Carlos has two big goals: make training simple and accessible to everyone and to be seen as an educator, not just a trainer. He enjoys his work and wants others to experience the joy of having a well-behaved dog.

He has a strong presence on social media and digital platforms where he shares his knowledge and experience with dog owners from all over the world.

Carlos says that there is no magic trick when it comes to training dogs, but understanding their behavior and being patient enough. Losing patience can lead to a negative experience for both the owner and the dog.

Carlos realised that using apps can help him make the training process easier for the owners, as he can provide feedback and guidance in real time, while also being able to keep track of the progress of the dog and the owner. Being able to use tele-training is also a big plus for him, as he can reach people from all over the world. Seeing the health card of the dog will also help him understand the dog's behavior better and adapt the training sessions accordingly.

Scenarios

Carlos could conduct a one-on-one outdoor training session focusing on leash manners, he can show the owner exactly how to correct the dog's behavior and give feedback in real time. The owner can also ask questions and get advice on how to handle certain situations. Carlos can use the AR feature to help a new dog owner who struggles with leash pulling by overlaying visual cues for the owner to follow. The owner will end up being more confident and the dog will begin to show improvement in its behavior.

During a training session, if Carlos notices that a dog seems unwell or not responding well to training cues, Carlos can open the dog's health card on the "GUDBOI" app to check if there's been a recent medication change or anything that could affect the dog's mood. He can then advise the owner to consult with a vet before continuing the training session.

If a dog struggles with a particular skill, Carlos can check the dog's progress on the app and adjust the training plan accordingly. He can also provide additional resources and tips to help the owner practice at home, which will result in the dog improving this skill over time.
